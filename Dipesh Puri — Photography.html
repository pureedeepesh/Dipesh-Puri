About the photographer

Dipesh
Puri

Dipesh Puri is a Toronto-based photographer whose love for the craft grew organically as he strolled through the city, drawn to everything interesting he found along the way — the people, the streets, the buildings, the alleyways, the parks.

What began as quiet curiosity quickly became a calling. Over three years, that curiosity expanded into shooting events, portraits, and light video work. Every frame is an invitation to look closer at the world we often rush past.

His work is rooted in the belief that every street has a story, and every city has a soul worth documenting.
